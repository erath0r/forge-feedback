# FORGE Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

## Introduction

FORGE ("we", "our", or "us") is a strength training and workout tracking application designed to help users track workouts, monitor progress, and manage fitness goals.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

* Email address
* Authentication information provided through our login system

### Workout Data

FORGE stores information that you voluntarily enter, including:

* Workout templates
* Exercises
* Sets, reps, and weights
* Workout history
* Personal records (PRs)
* Goals and progression data

### Device and Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information necessary to operate the application, including:

* App version
* Device type
* Crash and diagnostic information
* Authentication session information

## Health and Fitness Data

FORGE may integrate with Apple Health in future versions.

If Apple Health integration is enabled:

* Data is only accessed with your explicit permission.
* You may revoke access at any time through Apple Health settings.
* We only access data necessary for the features you choose to use.

FORGE does not sell health or fitness information.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* Provide workout tracking features
* Synchronize data between devices
* Calculate progress metrics
* Generate workout recommendations
* Improve app functionality
* Diagnose bugs and technical issues

## Data Storage

Workout and account data may be stored:

* Locally on your device
* In secure cloud infrastructure used by FORGE

We use authentication and access controls designed to ensure users can only access their own data.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only when necessary to:

* Provide core application functionality
* Comply with legal obligations
* Protect the security and integrity of the service

## Third-Party Services

FORGE may use third-party services such as:

* Supabase (authentication and cloud database services)
* Apple services (Apple Health, App Store, TestFlight)
* Analytics or crash reporting tools (if enabled in future versions)

Each third-party service is governed by its own privacy policy.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

* Access your data
* Correct inaccurate information
* Request deletion of your account and associated data
* Export your data

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

FORGE is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

## Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the FORGE feedback repository or contact the developer through the channels provided in the app.
